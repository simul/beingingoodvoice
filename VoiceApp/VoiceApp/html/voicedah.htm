Voiced AH

Finally, check your posture and your relaxed, open jaw.

Play the audio, as Flloyd talks you through the Voiced AH.

Keeping your throat open to allow the breath to flow in, and then out on an AH sound.

Begin on a note somewhere in the middle of your range, your normal speaking range, and slide down in pitch until it is somewhere low, but still easy to sustain.

Stay on the final note for a beat or two. Finish making the sound, but keep your mouth open for another couple of beats.

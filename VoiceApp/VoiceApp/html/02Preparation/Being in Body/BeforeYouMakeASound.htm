Before You Make a Sound

Before you begin to warm up your voice, warm up your whole body. Bounce gently on your feet, hop, move your arms, jog or dance about for a minute or two.

Use the Stand Easy and Move Easy exercises to get used to balancing your whole body in relation to gravity, so that you stand, sit and move with ease.

When you stand with ease, you can move with ease and speak with ease. Being aware of your physical state puts you in control of it.

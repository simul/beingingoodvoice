Why Voice Matters

Your voice is you, audible. When you use your voice, you express yourself and, in doing so, you communicate something of yourself to the world beyond yourself. As a performer or a presenter, you invite your listener to understand the world the way you see it, to share in the world of ideas or imagination you are expressing

How you sound (the vocal qualities that carry your words) has as much, if not more influence on how your are perceived as the way you look and move. Your vocal behaviours impact upon your listeners just as body language does - imperceptibly, subtly and profoundly.

Working on your voice is the only way to improve your speech.

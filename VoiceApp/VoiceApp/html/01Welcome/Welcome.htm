Welcome to the Being in Voice VoiceApp

Working with your voice is an art and a craft, not a technical exercise, and therefore it’s about expression, and communication. To learn about your voice is to learn to express, and to share.

Voice is an act of generousity

Drawing upon principles for training professional actors, this app will help you to release your vocal potential, allowing you to communicate easily, effectively and powerfully in stressful situations, such as presentations, seminars, public speaking or teaching.

Developing your voice is the only way to improve your speech.

Use the Preparation exercises to train yourself to adjust your body as you speak, to be aware of unnecessary and inappropriate tension in the body that may impede your freedom of movement and that certainly reduces the range of tone and colour in your vocal sound.

Build yourself up to a full-bodied, generous vocal sound, first with the Mini Vocal Warmup, then extending it into the Vocal Maintenance program.

As your voice grows, you improve your clarity of speech by training the specific organs of articulation (lips, tongue, teeth, jaw and palates) to be flexible, powerful and agile.

However, a well-balanced voice, or vocal sound is essential in the first place, to have a great range of vocal qualities, colours and tones available to be shaped into speech sounds.

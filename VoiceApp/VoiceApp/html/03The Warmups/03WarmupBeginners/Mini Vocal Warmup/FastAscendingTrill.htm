Fast Ascending Trill

Can you blow bubbles through your lips? Brrrrrr. Or make a trilled R sound Rrrrrrr (like a Scottish, or Italian Rrrrrr)?

• Begin low-ish in your range (no strain) and slide quite quickly up in pitch to somewhere high-ish (without straining). Allow the sound to float away freely as soon as you get to the squeak.

• Trill from low to high two or three times, beginning a little lower, and finishing a little higher each time.

• Allow the breath in softly, easily and silently each time.

As you become more comfortable with the trill over time, make it last longer, i.e. take more time to get from low to high, and enjoy the journey.

Stay in your easy pitch range, you do not need to be as high, or as low as I am, or you might be higher or lower. Whatever it is, it is interesting.

Fast Ascending Trill

Can you blow bubbles through your lips? Brrrrrr! Or make a trilled R sound Rrrrrrr (like a Scottish, or Italian Rrrrrr)?

If you struggle with either of these, don't panic! You can learn how to do them. It just takes time, and patience.

The important thing to remember is that everyone CAN do them, but some people have to work at it.

You can slide up and down on a hum for your warmup, while you continue to practice blowing those bubbles, and rolling (or trilling) those rrrr's.

Begin low-ish in your range (no strain) and slide quite quickly up in pitch to somewhere high-ish (without straining). Remember, this is a warmup, not training.

Trill from low to high two or three times, beginning a little lower, and finishing a little higher each time.

Remember to allow the breath in softly, easily and silently each time.

As you become more comfortable with the trill over time, make it last longer, i.e. take more time to get from low to high, and enjoy the journey.

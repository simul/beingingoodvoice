Finish with Focus

To conclude your warmup, here are two exercises from the Russian style of voice training. They will keep you focussed, energised and ready to undertake whatever vocal challenges the day brings you.

The MAH exercise begins with a hum, opens into the AH sound, and floats freely away into the room.

SEA MOON sets up a swaying motion in the body, but with the sound always coming from the centre of the body.

• Allow breath (always!) to drop into the body easily, silently, quickly, whenever you need it.

• Visualise the Sea out to your right, the Moon rising to your left, and use your arms to indicate the size and distance of your vision

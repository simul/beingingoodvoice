The Hum

Continue to warm up your voice by humming gently, and smoothly.

Play the audio, as Flloyd talks you through 
the Hum.

Begin in the middle of your range and slide up and down in pitch, a little higher and a little lower each time.

Be easy, play with the sound. If your voice cracks and wobbles your challenge is to eventually do it smoothly. It takes practice and time for the vocal muscles to become strong and flexible.

Now you're ready to Trill!

Sighing AH

Give your sigh a little more vocal energy, so that it now sounds more like 'AH', but still very gently. This continues the process of stimulating blood flow to the whole of the larynx. It also requires more breath energy, so be vigilant in observing the easy, fast, silent inflow of air as you prepare to sigh it out on an 'AH' sound.
